FAQs — tied to the timeline

Rain, family legs and compression answers map to concrete days.

Day 3 pours rain — what moves indoors?

Expand Thyssen or Sorolla inside Day 3 · 17:30 optional museum slot; shorten Retiro to Crystal Palace photo only. If storms linger, steal unfinished Prado wing time only if tickets still valid—never improvise both giants same afternoon.

Kids under 10 — where do we slim the spine?

Cut Reina Sofía to Guernica + sculpture patio (Day 2); swap Day 4 Toledo for half-day Retiro rowboats + zoo adjacency only if animal ethics align—otherwise Madrid cable-car viewpoints near Casa de Campo.

Weekend in Madrid only — credible trim?

Run Day 1 evening taper, full Day 2 museums, and morning half of Day 3 (Retiro → brunch); drop Toledo and Rastro. That’s a workable weekend in Madrid plan without five-day fiction.

Is Days 1–3 basically a Madrid 3 day itinerary?

Yes—core historic wedge + Prado/Reina spine + Retiro lungs form the canonical three days Madrid schedule. Treat Day 4 Toledo and Day 5 departure buffer as extensions.

Ultra-tight flight — steal time where?

Delete Day 5 Rastro; collapse Day 3 evening Chueca crawl to one reservation; never compress Prado morning security or Toledo return rail margin.
