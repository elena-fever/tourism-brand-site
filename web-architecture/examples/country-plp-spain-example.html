Getting around Spain

AVE high-speed rail links Madrid with Barcelona, Seville, Málaga and Valencia quickly—ideal for first trips without a car.

  • Rural Andalusia, northern villages and wine routes often suit a hire car.
  • Islands (Mallorca, Tenerife, Gran Canaria): domestic flights or ferries from mainland hubs.

Best season to visit Spain

Spring (April–June) and early autumn balance daylight, warmth and lighter crowds in major cities. Southern beaches peak July–August (heat + volume).

Summer travellers should plan indoor midday slots (museums) and book blockbuster sights early. For granular month-by-month answers per city, link out from City PDPs / thematic guides—not duplicated here.
